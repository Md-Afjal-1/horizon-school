Academic Facilities

We provide modern smart classrooms equipped with digital boards and interactive learning tools. Our fully equipped Physics, Chemistry, and Biology laboratories, advanced computer and robotics labs, language lab, audio-visual rooms, and a well-stocked library with digital resources ensure a strong academic foundation for every student.

FIND COURSE

Sports & Physical Facilities

Our campus includes spacious playgrounds, basketball and football grounds, cricket pitch, indoor games area, yoga and meditation sessions, and fitness activities. We promote physical strength, teamwork, and discipline through regular sports events and annual competitions.

Safety & Transportation

Student safety is our top priority. The campus is monitored with CCTV surveillance, trained security staff, fire safety systems, and a fully equipped medical room. We also provide safe and reliable transportation services with GPS-enabled school buses.

Technology Facilities

Our institution integrates technology-driven learning through high-speed internet access, smart classrooms, e-learning platforms, online classes, digital attendance systems, and student portals to ensure seamless communication and modern education standards.

Co-Curricular Facilities

We encourage creativity and talent through music, dance, drama, debate, art and craft activities, cultural programs, leadership development, and participation in competitions. Our co-curricular programs help students build confidence and overall personality development.

Other Important Facilities

The campus provides hygienic cafeteria services, clean drinking water, well-maintained washrooms, counseling support, parent-teacher interaction sessions, scholarship programs, and a supportive learning environment designed for student growth and success.
